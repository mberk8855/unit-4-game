Crystals Collector Game!


The Computer will give you a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the Computer's number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the Computer's number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game begins, the game will change the value of each crystal.
